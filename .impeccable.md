## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are general consumers checking whether a supplement, nutrient, or ingredient is safe for them alongside current medications, conditions, or life stage. The core job is to get a clean answer quickly, then open deeper evidence only when needed.

### Brand Personality
Clean, professional, restrained. The interface should feel orderly and dependable, with specialized knowledge presented in a way that stays easy to scan and never feels intimidating.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light-mode only. Favor a minimal, crisp product feel closer to ChatGPT than a decorative editorial site: neutral surfaces, clear hierarchy, compact controls, strong readability, and very limited accent use. Avoid AI-sounding copy, flashy gradients, ornamental layouts, glassmorphism, and anything that feels overdesigned or generically "AI SaaS."

### Design Principles
1. Put the most important answer first.
2. Keep primary screens short, clean, and immediately scannable.
3. Hide secondary detail behind clear expand or drill-down patterns.
4. Use layout, spacing, and typography for hierarchy before color or effects.
5. Explain professional knowledge in plain, compact language without dumbing it down.
