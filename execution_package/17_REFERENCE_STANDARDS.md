# 방법론 기준 문헌

실행 시 최신 버전과 공식 페이지를 다시 확인한다.

1. Page MJ, McKenzie JE, Bossuyt PM, et al. The PRISMA 2020 statement: an updated guideline for reporting systematic reviews. BMJ. 2021;372:n71. doi:10.1136/bmj.n71.
2. Rethlefsen ML, Kirtley S, Waffenschmidt S, et al. PRISMA-S: an extension to the PRISMA Statement for Reporting Literature Searches in Systematic Reviews. Syst Rev. 2021;10:39. doi:10.1186/s13643-020-01542-z.
3. Higgins JPT, Thomas J, Chandler J, et al., editors. Cochrane Handbook for Systematic Reviews of Interventions. Current online version.
4. Sterne JAC, Savović J, Page MJ, et al. RoB 2: a revised tool for assessing risk of bias in randomised trials. BMJ. 2019;366:l4898.
5. ROBINS-I / ROBINS-E official risk-of-bias tools. Use the current official version available at execution.
6. GRADE Working Group. Official guidance for certainty of evidence and evidence-to-decision.
7. Campbell M, McKenzie JE, Sowden A, et al. Synthesis without meta-analysis (SWiM) in systematic reviews: reporting guideline. BMJ. 2020;368:l6890.
8. McGowan J, Sampson M, Salzwedel DM, et al. PRESS Peer Review of Electronic Search Strategies: 2015 Guideline Statement. J Clin Epidemiol. 2016;75:40-46.
9. Shea BJ, Reeves BC, Wells G, et al. AMSTAR 2. BMJ. 2017;358:j4008.
10. van de Schoot R, de Bruin J, Schram R, et al. An open source machine learning framework for efficient and transparent systematic reviews. Nat Mach Intell. 2021;3:125-133.

학교 내부 기준은 `전공심화실습 자료집 및 학위논문 작성법.pdf`를 따른다. 내부 자료의 PRISMA 2009 표기는 최신 PRISMA 2020으로 갱신하되 학교 형식은 유지한다.
